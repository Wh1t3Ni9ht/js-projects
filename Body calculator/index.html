Body Calculator

BMI
Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on your weight in relation to your height, and applies to most adult men and women aged 18 and over. For children aged 2 and over, BMI percentile is the best assessment of body fat.
BMR
Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is an estimate of how many calories you'd burn if you were to do nothing but rest for 24 hours. It represents the minimum amount of energy needed to keep your body functioning.
